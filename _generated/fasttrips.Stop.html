fasttrips.Stop¶

class fasttrips.Stop(input_dir)[source]¶

Stop class.

One instance represents all of the Stops as well as their transfer links.

Stores stop information in Stop.stops_df, an instance of pandas.DataFrame and transfer link information in Stop.transfers_df, another instance of pandas.DataFrame.
