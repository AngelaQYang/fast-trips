fasttrips.Trip¶

class fasttrips.Trip(input_dir, output_dir, gtfs_schedule, today, is_child_process, stops, routes, prepend_route_id_to_trip_id)[source]¶

Trip class.

One instance represents all of the transit vehicle trips.

Stores Trip information in Trip.trips_df, an instance of pandas.DataFrame and stop time information in Trip.stop_times_df, another instance of pandas.DataFrame.

Also stores Vehicle information in Trips.vehicles_df and Service Calendar information in Trips.service_df
